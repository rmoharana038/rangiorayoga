Private Yoga Sessions

Padma is pleased to be able to offer private one-one-one yoga sessions. This sessions can be conducted at the Rangiora Yoga studio on High Street or in the comfort of your own home.

Private sessions differ from group classes because they allow Padma time to guide you in and out of every pose correctly in order to enhance your experience and avoid injury.

Private yoga lessons are great for those who are new to yoga but reluctant to try a group class, as well as for experienced yogis who want to perfect their craft with some extra guidance from Padma. Private consultations are also ideal for those with previous injuries. Padma is able to formulate an individual yoga plan that works around your injuries and compliments your rehabilitation goals.

Private sessions are available Monday to Friday from 7.30am to 9am, 10.45am to 12pm and 1pm to 5pm. Sessions are also available on Monday and Thursday evenings. For more information contact Rangiora Yoga today!

Bookings are required for private sessions. You can check their Facebook page for updates.
