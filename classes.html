Class Descriptions

Restorative Yoga

A slow and deep stretch for relaxation, suitable for beginners. (45 mins)

Hatha Yoga

A dynamic practice that links breath with movement. Suitable for all levels.

Vinyasa Flow

A gentle yet challenging practice that focuses on alignment and breath control.

Astanga Vinyasa

A deeply relaxing practice that uses props to support the body in gentle poses.

Prenatal Yoga

A vigorous and athletic practice that follows a specific sequence of postures.

Yin Yoga

A slow-paced, meditative practice that targets the deep connective tissues.

Prenatal Yoga

A gentle and supportive practice for expectant mothers.
