Private Yoga Sessions

Padma is pleased to offer **private one-on-one yoga sessions**, designed to provide personalized guidance and support. These sessions can be conducted at the **Rangiora Yoga studio on High Street** or in the comfort of your **own home**.

Why Choose Private Sessions?

  • **Personalized Attention:** Unlike group classes, private sessions allow Padma to guide you in and out of every pose correctly, enhancing your experience and minimizing injury risk.
  • **Ideal for Beginners:** Perfect for those new to yoga who may feel reluctant to join a group class.
  • **Advanced Guidance:** Experienced yogis can perfect their craft with tailored guidance from Padma.
  • **Injury Rehabilitation:** Ideal for individuals with previous injuries. Padma is able to formulate an individual yoga plan that works around your specific needs and complements your rehabilitation goals.

Availability:

  • **Monday to Friday:** 7:30 AM - 9:00 AM, 10:45 AM - 12:00 PM, and 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  • **Monday and Thursday Evenings:** Sessions also available.

For more information or to book a session, please **contact Rangiora Yoga today!**

Bookings are required for private sessions. You can check their Facebook page for updates.
