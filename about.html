About Rangiora Yoga

Our Story

Padma has been teaching yoga for over 15 years. Originally from India, she has been living in New Zealand since 2016 and now offers yoga classes in Rangiora, North Canterbury.

Padma credits yoga with helping her control her asthma and improve her overall health. She holds a post-graduate diploma in yoga therapy and a PhD in Yoga.

Our Mission

Our mission is to share the transformative power of yoga, helping individuals find balance, strength, and inner peace through a practice that is safe, sustainable, and tailored to their individual needs.
