About Rangiora Yoga

Our Story

For Dr Padmavati Maharana, yoga is more than just exercise; it's a way of life, guided by her personal mantra: “Live yoga, love yoga and laugh yoga.”

Affectionately known as Padma, she brings over 15 years of teaching experience. Originally from Kanpur Village in Odisha, India, she moved to New Zealand in 2016, where she has since taught yoga in Wellington and Canterbury.

Now settled in Rangiora, Padma is excited to offer classes at her own studio on Victoria Street, North Canterbury. She finds immense satisfaction in teaching, believing that yoga has no age limit and can significantly enhance health, wellbeing, and happiness through its various aspects, including postures, breathing, meditation, and cleansing techniques.

Padma's personal journey with yoga began over 15 years ago as a means to manage her health. She credits yoga with dramatically reducing her frequent asthma attacks and colds, bringing her asthma completely under control.

Her extensive qualifications include a yoga instructors course, a post-graduate diploma in yoga therapy, and a PhD in Yoga titled Effect of Integrated Yoga Module on Leadership Development.

Our Mission

Our mission is to share the transformative power of yoga, helping individuals find balance, strength, and inner peace through a practice that is safe, sustainable, and tailored to their individual needs.
