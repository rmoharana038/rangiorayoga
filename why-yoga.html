Why Yoga?

The increasing popularity of yoga is due to its numerous benefits. Physical benefits include increased flexibility, muscle strength and tone, improved respiration, energy, and vitality, balanced metabolism, weight reduction, improved cardiovascular and circulatory health, enhanced athletic performance, and protection from injury.

Beyond physical benefits, regular yoga practice is also beneficial for mental well-being. Research indicates that it helps reduce stress by activating the parasympathetic nervous system, which is the body's rest system. This activation allows the body to release tension and muscles to relax, leading to de-stressing and relaxation for both mind and body. Consistent practice can also improve one's ability to handle stressful events.
