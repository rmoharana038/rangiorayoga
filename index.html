About us

For Dr Padmavati Maharana, yoga is not just exercise – it is a way of life. Her personal mantra is “Live yoga, love yoga and laugh yoga”. Affectionately known as Padma to her friends and students, the 41 year-old has been teaching yoga for more than 15 years. Originally from India, she has been living in New Zealand since 2016 and now offers yoga classes in Rangiora, North Canterbury. Padma credits yoga with helping her control her asthma and improve her overall health. She holds a post-graduate diploma in yoga therapy and a PhD in Yoga.
